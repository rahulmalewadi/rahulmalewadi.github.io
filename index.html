About Me

I am From Pune - a vibrant & cultural hub of Maharashtra,situated roughly 200 miles from Mumbai. As a school kid, my interest in automobiles led me to pursue a degree in engineering.

In 2017, I started my educational journey as a mechanical engineering student at the prestigious Savitribai Phule Pune University (Pune). There I had a great interaction with the latest technologies helping to tshape the world. I finished my engineering in July, 2020, and started my professional journey.

Since August 2020, I worked at a startup called Talentedge.in there I was exposed to various new technologies and that really changed the game for me. I worked there for almost 8 months till a bigger opportunity knocked my door. Since June 2021 , I started working for the consulting giant Capgemini FS and there I had opportunity to continue my career as a DevSecOps Engineer , I am currently working for a fortune 500 client for providing highly effiecient devops solutions on various technologies like jenkins, github actions, docker, aks,pcf & many more. Apart from my professional work I constantly work on developing new web applications as a hobby , working with Java , Javascript related technologies so if you are interested please feel free to contact me for a open-source collabration project or even a entrepreneurship idea.
